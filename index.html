Tropic Thunder

Action / Comedy | 2008

Synopsis

A group of self-absorbed actors set out to make the most expensive war movie ever. When their frustrated director drops them in a real jungle to capture their "raw" performances, they must rely on their acting skills to survive very real dangers.

The cast, including a committed method actor and a potty-mouthed comedian, stumble into a drug-running gang's territory. What starts as a film production quickly becomes a mission of survival, blurring the lines between fiction and reality in the most explosive ways.

Did You Know?

  • Tom Cruise's role as Les Grossman was his idea, including the bald cap and bizarre dance moves.
  • Robert Downey Jr. stayed in character as Kirk Lazarus even between takes.
  • The opening napalm explosion is one of the largest practical explosions ever in a comedy.
  • Ben Stiller got the idea for the movie while filming "Empire of the Sun" in 1987.
